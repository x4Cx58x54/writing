Catalan Number

The Catalan numbers are a sequence of integers named after the mathematician Eugène Charles Catalan.

The \(n\)th Catalan number is

\[ C_n = \frac{1}{n+1}\binom{2n}{n}, \quad n \ge 0. \tag 1 \]
